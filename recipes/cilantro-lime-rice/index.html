Cilantro lime rice

Ingredients

  • White onion
  • Garlic, smashed & chopped
  • Lime (cested!)
  • Rice (Basmati or brown)
  • ground coriander
  • cilantro, chopped
  • scallions, sliced (garnish)

Directions

Like you'd do with a risotto,

  2. add rice, saute 2'ish, stir
